About

For Technology class, 5A students were asked to create their own webpages.
In preparation for this, they learned how the Internet functions, the workings of the World Wide Web, and how to write a document in HTML and CSS.

Their assignment was to create a webpage for a fake company or store from their own imagination.
They did an absolutely fantastic job tackling this fun yet difficult task, and this is the product of their work!

In this website, you will be able to see the code that each student wrote. Just choose a webpage from the homepage menu, and once you enter, click on the HTML icon found at the top right corner of the page.
