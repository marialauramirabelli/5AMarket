ICE CREAMETTI


Are you bored of the traditional, plain ice cream flavours that all the ice-cream shops have? Are you looking for exciting combinations that will surprise your taste buds?

Introducing Ice Creametti, a new ice-cream shop here in Costa Rica that stays away from plain flavours like vainilla (boring!), chocolate (yuck!), and strawberry (ugh!).

Ice Creametti is the perfect place to try exotic and unique ice creams!



Menu

  • Mac and Cheese Cone: A giant cone made of pasta with two scoops of cheddar ice cream and parmesan cheese as the topping
  • Bolognese Sundae: Delicious pasta ice cream with tomato sauce and meat topping
  • Carbonara Milkshake: Egg and cheese ice cream blended with milk and yummy chunks of bacon


Where to find us

We are located in Costa Rica, in front of Saint Mary School (the students there REALLY love our products!).

You can also order our ice cream through Uber Eats, Glovo, and Rappi.
